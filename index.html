Work, Services, and Impact

I design and build simple, modern websites and small tools, and I lead student-run events and initiatives from planning to wrap-up. My focus is clear goals, smooth execution, and results you can feel: better visibility, easier workflows, and stronger communities. Whatever the scope, I keep it practical, dependable, and impact-driven.

Web Design

I design clean, friendly pages for portfolios and small businesses. I start with your goals, keep layouts simple, test on phones and desktops, and improve the design based on feedback. I’m still growing, so I focus on clarity over fancy effects.

Project Management and Student Leadership

I lead student-run events and initiatives from planning to wrap-up by setting clear goals, building teams, and aligning tasks and timelines. I coordinate volunteers, partners, and org officers, handle logistics and simple budgets, and keep everyone updated with clear messages. On event day I manage flow and troubleshoot calmly. After, I gather feedback and share takeaways so the next project runs even better.

Web Development

I turn ideas into working websites and basic tools. I keep things straightforward, write clear notes, and make sure the project is easy to use and update. I value speed, accessibility, and continuous improvement.
